Dragon Ball Z

The epic tale of Goku and his friends

Here's a timeline of Dragon Ball Z:

  • 1989 - Dragon Ball Z anime series premieres in Japan

  • 1996 - Dragon Ball Z ends with 291 episodes

  • 1997 - Dragon Ball Z premieres in the United States

  • 2003 - Dragon Ball Z video games become popular worldwide

  • 2009 - Dragon Ball Z Kai, a remastered version, is released

  • 2013 - Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods movie is released

  • 2015 - Dragon Ball Z: Resurrection 'F' movie is released

  • 2018 - Dragon Ball Super: Broly movie is released

  • 2021 - Dragon Ball Super continues the saga with new arcs
